CHANCE

1 Advance to GO. Collect $200 

2 Advance to Illinois Ave (last red). If you pass GO, collect $200. 

3 Advance to St. Charles Place (first purple). If you pass GO, collect $200. 

4 Advance token to nearest utility (Bruiser Camp). If unowned, you may buy it from the Bank. If owned, throw dice and pay owner a total 10 (ten) times the amount thrown.

5 Advance to the nearest Railroad (Boss Camp). If unowned, you may buy it from the Bank. If owned, pay owner twice the rent to which they are otherwise entitled. If Railroad is unowned, you may buy it from the Bank. 

6 Bank pays you dividend of $50

7 Get out of Jail Free. This card may be kept until needed, or traded/sold. 

8 Go back 3 spaces 

9 Go to Jail. Go directly to Jail. 

10 Make general repairs on all your property: For each house pay $25, for each hotel pay $100

11 Take a trip to Reading Railroad (first railroad)

12 Take a walk on the Boardwalk. Advance token to Boardwalk. 

13 You have been elected Chairman of the Board. Pay each player $50

14 Your building loan matures. Collect $150

7 Grand Opera Night. Collect $50 from every player for opening night seats. 

8 Holiday fund matures. Collect $100 

9 Income tax refund. Collect $20

11 Life insurance matures. Collect $100 

12 Hospital fees. Pay $50 

13 School fees. Pay $50 

14 Receive $25 consultancy fee. 

15 You are assessed for street repairs. Pay $40 per house and $115 per hotel you own.
